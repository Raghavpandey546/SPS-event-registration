About the Event

The IEEE Student Chapter is pleased to invite you to *B.U.I.L.D – Building Reliable Infrastructure & Deployment in Global Environments.

This is not just another workshop session — it is an opportunity to gain real-world exposure to how applications are deployed, managed, and debugged in professional environments. Participants will get the chance to work on practical deployment tasks, solve real-time issues, and experience the kind of challenges developers face in industry.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Deploy your own local projects using Vercel and AWS EC2
  • Understand and implement CI/CD pipelines using GitHub
  • Learn real-world deployment strategies and debugging techniques
  • Gain practical exposure to hosting and managing applications online

The workshop will also include interactive activities such as team-based debugging games, scenario-based deployment challenges, time-bound technical problem-solving tasks, and collaborative project deployment sessions to make the learning experience more engaging and practical.

In addition, there will be a brief guidance session to help students understand suitable technical domains and career pathways based on their interests and future goals.

Certificates of Participation will be provided to all attendees.
